Creating our website, Odyssia

Odyssia is a website created to help travelers get good recommendations about their destinations as well as giving them the opportunity to share their tips and advice with the world. It is created by two students, Jorge and Konstantina, to give everyone the chance to get the most out of their travels. Do not hesitate to get in touch!
